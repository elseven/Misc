Jump into Nature in Alpine Helen-White County, Ga.: rafting, tubing and fishing the Chattahoochee River; hiking Ana Ruby Falls and the trails at Unicoi State Park; horseback riding; mountain biking; grubbing for gold; exploring the mines; playing adventure golf and more. Alpine Helen-White County goes full speed ahead into outdoor fun, offering visitors a variety of outdoor adventures. Meal time is an adventure, too, with an internationally flavored dining scene that includes everything from wine country cuisine to country cooking, from schnitzel to smoked trout to tacos to teas.
